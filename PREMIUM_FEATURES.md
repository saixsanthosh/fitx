## 📐 UI Specifications

### Spacing System
- **4dp**: Tight spacing
- **8dp**: Default spacing
- **16dp**: Card padding
- **24dp**: Section spacing
- **32dp**: Large spacing

### Corner Radius
- **8dp**: Small elements
- **16dp**: Medium cards
- **24dp**: Large cards
- **28dp**: Buttons
- **32dp**: Hero cards

### Typography Scale
- **12sp**: Caption

- **16sp**: Body
- **18sp**: Subtitle
- **20sp**: Title small
- **24sp**: Title
- **32sp**: Headline
- **56sp**: Display

### Elevation
- **0dp**: Flat
- **2dp**: Pressed

- **8dp**: Raised
